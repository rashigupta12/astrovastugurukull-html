Frequently Asked Questions

Does this course cover all 12 Rashis in detail or just a few?
This course covers all 12 Rashis comprehensively — their ruling planets, elemental nature, core personality traits, strengths, weaknesses, and compatibility patterns. Each Rashi gets dedicated focused content.
What is the difference between a Sun sign and a Moon sign Rashi?
This is one of the key concepts covered in the course. In Vedic astrology, your Rashi typically refers to your Moon sign — the sign the Moon was in at your birth — which differs from the Western Sun sign system. The course clearly explains both and their significance.
Is this course standalone or do I need to take the Beginner's Astrology Course first?
The All About Rashis course is designed as a standalone module that can be studied independently. However, combining it with the Beginner's Astrology Course gives you a much richer and more complete learning experience.
Will I learn how Rashis affect relationships and compatibility?
Yes, the course includes a section on inter-Rashi compatibility — which signs are naturally harmonious, which create friction, and why. This is extremely useful for understanding personal and professional relationships through an astrological lens.
How is this course delivered — are there videos, PDFs, or live classes?
The course is delivered through pre-recorded video lectures supported by downloadable PDF notes and reference material. You can access everything at your convenience on any device, at any time after enrollment.
