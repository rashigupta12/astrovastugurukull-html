Frequently Asked Questions

I have never studied numerology before — will this course be easy to follow?
Yes, this course is built from the ground up for complete beginners. You will start with what numbers mean in numerology, how to calculate core numbers like Life Path, Destiny, and Soul Urge, and what they reveal about a person's character and life journey.
Which system of numerology is taught — Chaldean, Pythagorean, or Indian?
The course primarily focuses on the Chaldean numerology system, which is considered the most ancient and accurate. We also introduce key elements of the Indian numerology tradition, giving you a well-rounded foundational understanding.
Can I use numerology to understand my own life purpose after this course?
Yes, one of the most exciting takeaways is calculating and interpreting your own core numbers — Life Path, Expression Number, and Birthday Number — which together reveal your strengths, challenges, and life purpose.
How long does the beginner numerology course take to complete?
The course is compact and focused, designed to be completed in 2 to 3 weeks at a relaxed pace. It is an excellent starting point and pairs naturally with our Name Correction and Mobile Number Correction courses.
Is there a certificate awarded after completing this course?
Yes, upon successful completion of the course you will receive a certificate from Astro Vastu Gurukul, which you can use to demonstrate your knowledge and begin basic numerology consultations or readings.
